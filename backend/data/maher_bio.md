## Key Portfolio Projects & Case Studies

### 1. Al Rajhi Bank Payroll Revamp
- **Role**: Senior Product Designer (AR/EN).
- **Problem**: Enterprise payroll interfaces were fragmented, leading to user friction and slow transactions.
- **Approach**: Redesigned the payroll flows by prioritizing transaction speed, clarifying tabular data, and creating a cohesive bilingual interface.
- **Outcome**: Delivered as part of a broader platform revamp that resulted in +47% online account openings and +81% e-business transactions. (Case study linked externally to Behance).
- **Slug**: `alrajhi-bank-payroll`

### 2. Sanarte Mobile App
- **Role**: Lead Product Designer (mobile).
- **Problem**: Remote workers struggled to maintain deep focus due to stress and distraction; the client needed a research-backed soundscape tool.
- **Approach**: Conducted intensive user research, competitor reviews, and usability tests to design a mobile audio dashboard.
- **Outcome**: Made it 30% easier for remote workers to listen to focus-boosting sounds, achieving 100% usability task completion.
- **Slug**: `sanarte`

### 3. LFG App
- **Role**: Product Designer (mobile).
- **Problem**: The Milt Olin Foundation needed a habit-tracking application that kept drivers motivated to avoid phone distractions on roads.
- **Approach**: Built a gamification structure around the eight core drives of the Octalysis Gamification method, designing milestones, scarcity rewards, leaderboards, and digital collectibles.
- **Outcome**: Made the safe-driving journey 20% more engaging for users tracking safe driving habit streaks.
- **Slug**: `lfg`
